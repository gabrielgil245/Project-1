Hello! My name is Gabriel Gil, a single person indie developer, determined to provide fun, quality games for the enjoyment of others. The quality of my games is measured in the respect and consideration of all stakeholders that may improve the fun and enjoyment from my video games.

At the age of five, I was at awe to the visuals and fun that are video games. The first game I recall was watching my brother play The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time. And from then on, I have been entralled to the world of video games.

Twenty years later, with an education in business, I am dedicated to to learn game engines, like Unity, to share my enjoyment in video games with others. While I may have plenty of experience in video games, I am open to feedback that may improve the quality and enjoyment in my video games.

If you have any suggestions to my current or future projects, please feel free to contact me, using the Contact tab. Your constructive feedback is always welcomed! Thank you, and thank you for playing my games!
